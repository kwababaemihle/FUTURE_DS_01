# Superstore Sales Analysis – Task 1

## Internship
Data Science & Analytics Internship – Future Interns

## Objective
Analyze business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-selling products, high-value categories, and regional performance.

## Dataset
Superstore Sales Dataset (Kaggle)

## Tools Used
Python
Pandas
Matplotlib
Google Colab

## Analysis Performed
- Revenue trends over time
- Top-selling products
- Sales by category
- Regional sales performance

## Key Insights
- Technology is the highest revenue-generating category.
- The West region produces the highest total sales.
- Sales increase significantly toward the end of the year.
- A small number of products generate a large portion of total revenue.

## Recommendations
- Increase marketing for top-performing categories.
- Focus expansion efforts in high-performing regions.
- Use seasonal promotions during peak months.
